## Módulo 15: Rutina Diaria

### 🎯 Objetivo
Describir tu rutina diaria y hablar sobre actividades frecuentes.

### 🧠 Vocabulario: Actividades Diarias

| Inglés | Español | 🔊 |
|--------|---------|-----|
| wake up | despertarse | <AudioButton text="I wake up at 7" /> |
| get up | levantarse | <AudioButton text="I get up early" /> |
| take a shower | ducharse | <AudioButton text="I take a shower" /> |
| have breakfast | desayunar | <AudioButton text="I have breakfast at 8" /> |
| go to work | ir al trabajo | <AudioButton text="I go to work by bus" /> |
| have lunch | almorzar | <AudioButton text="I have lunch at 1" /> |
| get home | llegar a casa | <AudioButton text="I get home at 6" /> |
| have dinner | cenar | <AudioButton text="I have dinner at 8" /> |
| watch TV | ver televisión | <AudioButton text="I watch TV at night" /> |
| go to bed | ir a dormir | <AudioButton text="I go to bed at 11" /> |

### 🧩 Chunks: Frecuencia

| Expresión | Frecuencia | 🔊 |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| **always** | 100% | <AudioButton text="I always wake up early" /> |
| **usually** | 80% | <AudioButton text="I usually have coffee" /> |
| **often** | 60% | <AudioButton text="I often go to the gym" /> |
| **sometimes** | 40% | <AudioButton text="I sometimes eat out" /> |
| **rarely** | 20% | <AudioButton text="I rarely watch TV" /> |
| **never** | 0% | <AudioButton text="I never drink alcohol" /> |

### 🧩 Chunks: Expresiones de Tiempo

| Expresión | Uso | 🔊 |
|-----------|-----|-----|
| **in the morning** | por la mañana | <AudioButton text="in the morning" /> |
| **in the afternoon** | por la tarde | <AudioButton text="in the afternoon" /> |
| **in the evening** | por la noche (temprano) | <AudioButton text="in the evening" /> |
| **at night** | por la noche | <AudioButton text="at night" /> |
| **every day** | todos los días | <AudioButton text="every day" /> |
| **on weekends** | los fines de semana | <AudioButton text="on weekends" /> |

### 🎧 Listening: Rutina Modelo

> "I **usually wake up** at 7 in the morning. I **take a shower** and **have breakfast**. I **always** have coffee. Then I **go to work** at 8:30. I **have lunch** at 1 pm. I **usually get home** around 6. In the evening, I **sometimes** go to the gym. I **have dinner** at 8 and I **usually go to bed** at 11."

### 🗣️ Speaking: Describe tu Rutina

Practica diciendo:
1. <AudioButton text="I wake up at 7" />
2. <AudioButton text="I usually have coffee for breakfast" />
3. <AudioButton text="I go to work at 8" />
4. <AudioButton text="I get home at 6" />
5. <AudioButton text="I go to bed at 11" />

## Módulo 16: Compras y Dinero

### 🎯 Objetivo
Comunicarte efectivamente en tiendas y manejar transacciones básicas.

### 🧠 Vocabulario: Compras

| Inglés | Español |
|--------|---------|
| store/shop | tienda |
| price | precio |
| cash | efectivo |
| card | tarjeta |
| receipt | recibo |
| change | cambio |
| sale | oferta/rebaja |
| size | talla |
| color | color |
| bag | bolsa |

### 🧩 Chunks: Cliente

| Situación | Frase |
|-----------|-------|
| Buscando algo | **I'm looking for...** |
| Solo mirando | **I'm just looking, thanks** |
| Preguntando precio | **How much is this?** |
| Preguntando talla | **Do you have this in [size]?** |
| Preguntando color | **Do you have this in [color]?** |
| Probarse | **Can I try this on?** |
| Decidiendo | **I'll take it** |
| No comprando | **I'll think about it** |

### 🧩 Chunks: Pagar

| Situación | Frase |
|-----------|-------|
| Preguntar forma de pago | **Can I pay by card?** |
| Pagar en efectivo | **I'll pay in cash** |
| Pedir recibo | **Can I have the receipt?** |
| Pedir bolsa | **Can I have a bag?** |
| Agradecer | **Thank you. Have a nice day!** |

### 🧩 Chunks: Vendedor (para entender)

| Frase del vendedor | Significado |
|--------------------|-------------|
| "Can I help you?" | ¿Puedo ayudarte? |
| "Are you looking for something?" | ¿Buscas algo? |
| "What size do you need?" | ¿Qué talla necesitas? |
| "Would you like to try it on?" | ¿Quieres probártelo? |
| "Cash or card?" | ¿Efectivo o tarjeta? |
| "Here's your change" | Aquí está tu cambio |

### 🎧 Listening: Diálogo en Tienda

> A: Hi, can I help you?  
> B: Yes, **I'm looking for** a jacket.  
> A: What size?  
> B: Medium. **Do you have this in black?**  
> A: Yes, here you go.  
> B: **How much is it?**  
> A: It's $50.  
> B: Great. **I'll take it. Can I pay by card?**  
> A: Of course.

### 🗣️ Speaking: Practica el Diálogo

Practica estas frases:
1. **I'm looking for a [producto]**
2. **How much is this?**
3. **Do you have this in [talla/color]?**
4. **I'll take it**
5. **Can I pay by card?**

### 🔁 Práctica: Situaciones

| Situación | ¿Qué dices? |
|-----------|-------------|
| Quieres saber el precio | _____________ |
| Quieres otra talla | _____________ |
| Decides comprar | _____________ |
| Quieres pagar con tarjeta | _____________ |

### 🚀 Producción Final

Simula una compra completa: entra a la tienda, pregunta por un producto, pregunta el precio, y paga.

## Módulo 17: Comida y Restaurante

### 🎯 Objetivo
Pedir comida en un restaurante y expresar preferencias alimenticias.

### 🧠 Vocabulario: Restaurante

| Inglés | Español |
|--------|---------|
| menu | menú |
| table | mesa |
| waiter/waitress | mesero/a |
| order | pedido/ordenar |
| bill/check | cuenta |
| tip | propina |
| appetizer | entrada |
| main course | plato principal |
| dessert | postre |
| drink | bebida |

### 🧩 Chunks: Llegar al Restaurante

| Situación | Frase |
|-----------|-------|
| Reservación | **I have a reservation for [nombre]** |
| Sin reservación | **Table for two, please** |
| Preguntar espera | **How long is the wait?** |
| Pedir menú | **Can I have the menu, please?** |

### 🧩 Chunks: Ordenar

| Situación | Frase |
|-----------|-------|
| Listo para ordenar | **I'm ready to order** |
| Necesitas más tiempo | **Can I have a few more minutes?** |
| Ordenar | **I'll have the [plato]** |
| Preguntar recomendación | **What do you recommend?** |
| Pedir bebida | **Can I have a [bebida]?** |
| Algo más | **That's all, thank you** |

### 🧩 Chunks: Durante la Comida

| Situación | Frase |
|-----------|-------|
| Pedir algo más | **Can I have some more [agua/pan]?** |
| Problema con orden | **Excuse me, this isn't what I ordered** |
| Está delicioso | **This is delicious!** |
| Pedir la cuenta | **Can I have the bill/check, please?** |

### 🧩 Chunks: Expresar Gustos

| Expresión | Uso |
|-----------|-----|
| **I love...** | Me encanta... |
| **I like...** | Me gusta... |
| **I don't like...** | No me gusta... |
| **I hate...** | Odio... |
| **I'm allergic to...** | Soy alérgico a... |
| **I'm vegetarian** | Soy vegetariano |

### 🎧 Listening: Diálogo en Restaurante

> Waiter: Hi, are you ready to order?  
> Customer: Yes, **I'll have** the chicken pasta.  
> Waiter: Anything to drink?  
> Customer: **Can I have** a Coke, please?  
> Waiter: Sure. Anything else?  
> Customer: **That's all, thank you.**  
> [Later]  
> Customer: **Can I have the bill, please?**  
> Waiter: Of course. Here you go.

### 🗣️ Speaking: Practica Ordenar

Di estas frases:
1. **Table for two, please**
2. **Can I have the menu?**
3. **I'll have the [plato]**
4. **Can I have a glass of water?**
5. **Can I have the bill, please?**

### 🔁 Práctica: Completa el Diálogo

| Mesero dice | Tú respondes |
|-------------|--------------|
| Are you ready to order? | Yes, _____________ |
| Anything to drink? | _____________ |
| Anything else? | _____________ |

### 🚀 Producción Final

Simula una visita completa al restaurante: llegar, ordenar, pedir la cuenta.

## Módulo 18: Direcciones y Lugares

### 🎯 Objetivo
Preguntar y dar direcciones simples para llegar a lugares.

### 🧠 Vocabulario: Direcciones

| Inglés | Español |
|--------|---------|
| left | izquierda |
| right | derecha |
| straight | recto/derecho |
| corner | esquina |
| block | cuadra |
| next to | al lado de |
| across from | enfrente de |
| between | entre |
| near | cerca de |
| far | lejos |

### 🧠 Vocabulario: Lugares

| Inglés | Español |
|--------|---------|
| bank | banco |
| hospital | hospital |
| pharmacy | farmacia |
| supermarket | supermercado |
| gas station | gasolinera |
| bus stop | parada de bus |
| train station | estación de tren |
| airport | aeropuerto |
| hotel | hotel |
| parking lot | estacionamiento |

### 🧩 Chunks: Preguntar Direcciones

| Situación | Frase |
|-----------|-------|
| Preguntar ubicación | **Where is the [lugar]?** |
| Cómo llegar | **How do I get to [lugar]?** |
| Está cerca | **Is it far from here?** |
| Hay uno cerca | **Is there a [lugar] near here?** |
| Perdido | **I'm lost. Can you help me?** |

### 🧩 Chunks: Dar Direcciones

| Instrucción | Frase |
|-------------|-------|
| Ir recto | **Go straight** |
| Girar | **Turn left/right** |
| En la esquina | **Turn at the corner** |
| Cuadras | **Go two blocks** |
| Al lado de | **It's next to the [lugar]** |
| Enfrente | **It's across from the [lugar]** |

### 🧩 Chunks: Confirmar

| Situación | Frase |
|-----------|-------|
| Repetir | **So, I go straight and turn left?** |
| Agradecer | **Thank you so much!** |
| No entendiste | **Sorry, can you repeat that?** |

### 🎧 Listening: Pidiendo Direcciones

> A: Excuse me, **where is the bank?**  
> B: **Go straight** for two blocks, then **turn right**. It's **next to** the pharmacy.  
> A: So, straight and then right?  
> B: Yes, exactly.  
> A: **Is it far?**  
> B: No, it's about 5 minutes walking.  
> A: **Thank you so much!**

### 🗣️ Speaking: Practica Direcciones

Di estas frases:
1. **Excuse me, where is the bank?**
2. **How do I get to the train station?**
3. **Is there a pharmacy near here?**
4. **Go straight and turn left**
5. **It's next to the supermarket**

### 🔁 Práctica: Da Direcciones

| Desde tu casa, ¿cómo llego a...? | Direcciones |
|----------------------------------|-------------|
| El supermercado más cercano | Go _____, turn _____ |
| Una farmacia | It's _____ the _____ |
| El banco | Go _____ blocks, then _____ |

### 🚀 Producción Final

1. Pregunta cómo llegar a 3 lugares diferentes
2. Da direcciones desde tu casa a un lugar cercano

<Aside type="note">
**Tip:** Cuando no entiendas las direcciones, no tengas miedo de pedir que repitan: "Sorry, can you repeat that slowly?"
</Aside>
