## Módulo 3: Saludos y Expresiones Esenciales

### 🎯 Objetivo
Saludar, despedirse y usar expresiones de cortesía de forma natural.

### 🧠 Vocabulario: Saludos

| Formal | Informal | Muy informal |
|--------|----------|--------------|
| <AudioButton text="Good morning" /> | <AudioButton text="Hi" /> | <AudioButton text="Hey" /> |
| <AudioButton text="Good afternoon" /> | <AudioButton text="Hello" /> | <AudioButton text="What's up?" /> |
| <AudioButton text="Good evening" /> | <AudioButton text="How are you?" /> | <AudioButton text="How's it going?" /> |
| <AudioButton text="How do you do?" /> | <AudioButton text="Nice to see you" /> | <AudioButton text="Yo!" /> |

### 🧩 Chunks: Saludos y Respuestas

| Saludo | Respuesta natural | 🔊 |
|--------|-------------------|-----|
| Hi, how are you? | Good, thanks! And you? | <AudioButton text="Hi, how are you? Good, thanks! And you?" /> |
| What's up? | Not much. You? | <AudioButton text="What's up? Not much. You?" /> |
| How's it going? | Pretty good, thanks | <AudioButton text="How's it going? Pretty good, thanks" /> |
| How are you doing? | I'm doing well, thanks | <AudioButton text="How are you doing? I'm doing well, thanks" /> |
| Hey, what's new? | Nothing much, same old | <AudioButton text="Hey, what's new? Nothing much, same old" /> |

### 🎧 Listening: Diálogos de Saludo

**Diálogo 1: Informal** <AudioButton text="Hey! What's up? Not much. How's it going? Pretty good, thanks!" />
> A: Hey! What's up?  
> B: Not much. How's it going?  
> A: Pretty good, thanks!

**Diálogo 2: Formal** <AudioButton text="Good morning. How are you? I'm fine, thank you. And you? Very well, thanks." />
> A: Good morning. How are you?  
> B: I'm fine, thank you. And you?  
> A: Very well, thanks.

### 🗣️ Speaking: Practica estos intercambios

1. <AudioButton text="Hi! How are you? Good, thanks! And you?" />
2. <AudioButton text="What's up? Not much. You?" />
3. <AudioButton text="See you later! Bye! Take care!" />
4. <AudioButton text="Thanks a lot! No problem!" />

### 🔁 Práctica: Completa el diálogo

| A dice... | B responde... |
|-----------|---------------|
| Hi, how are you? | _____________ |
| What's up? | _____________ |
| Thanks! | _____________ |
| See you tomorrow! | _____________ |

### 🚀 Producción Final

Graba un mini diálogo de 30 segundos:
1. Saluda a alguien
2. Pregunta cómo está
3. Responde a su pregunta
4. Despídete

## Módulo 4: Presentaciones Personales

### 🎯 Objetivo
Presentarte y dar información básica sobre ti en 30 segundos.

### 🧠 Vocabulario: Información Personal

| Categoría | Palabras clave |
|-----------|----------------|
| Nombre | name, first name, last name |
| Edad | years old, age |
| Origen | from, country, city |
| Profesión | job, work, student |
| Estudios | study, major, school |

### 🧩 Chunks: Presentación

| Información | Frase | 🔊 |
|-------------|-------|-----|
| Nombre | My name is... / I'm... | <AudioButton text="My name is Carlos" /> |
| Edad | I'm [X] years old | <AudioButton text="I'm 25 years old" /> |
| Origen | I'm from [país/ciudad] | <AudioButton text="I'm from Mexico" /> |
| Residencia | I live in [ciudad] | <AudioButton text="I live in Guadalajara" /> |
| Profesión | I'm a [profesión] / I work as a... | <AudioButton text="I'm a software developer" /> |
| Estudios | I'm a student / I study [carrera] | <AudioButton text="I'm a student. I study engineering" /> |
| Trabajo | I work at [empresa] | <AudioButton text="I work at a tech company" /> |

### 🧩 Chunks: Preguntar Información

| Pregunta | Respuesta modelo | 🔊 |
|----------|------------------|-----|
| What's your name? | My name is Carlos / I'm Carlos | <AudioButton text="What's your name? My name is Carlos" /> |
| How old are you? | I'm 25 years old | <AudioButton text="How old are you? I'm 25 years old" /> |
| Where are you from? | I'm from Mexico | <AudioButton text="Where are you from? I'm from Mexico" /> |
| Where do you live? | I live in Guadalajara | <AudioButton text="Where do you live? I live in Guadalajara" /> |
| What do you do? | I'm a software developer | <AudioButton text="What do you do? I'm a software developer" /> |
| What do you study? | I study engineering | <AudioButton text="What do you study? I study engineering" /> |

### 🎧 Listening: Presentación Modelo

<AudioButton text="Hi! My name is Maria. I'm 28 years old and I'm from Colombia. I live in Bogotá. I'm a graphic designer and I work at a marketing company. Nice to meet you!" />

> "Hi! My name is Maria. I'm 28 years old and I'm from Colombia. I live in Bogotá. I'm a graphic designer and I work at a marketing company. Nice to meet you!"

### 🗣️ Speaking: Tu Presentación (30 segundos)

Estructura recomendada:
1. **Saludo:** Hi! / Hello!
2. **Nombre:** My name is...
3. **Edad:** I'm [X] years old
4. **Origen:** I'm from...
5. **Residencia:** I live in...
6. **Ocupación:** I'm a... / I work as... / I study...
7. **Cierre:** Nice to meet you!

### 🔁 Práctica: Completa tu información

| Pregunta | Tu respuesta |
|----------|--------------|
| What's your name? | My name is __________ |
| How old are you? | I'm __________ years old |
| Where are you from? | I'm from __________ |
| What do you do? | I'm a __________ |

### 🚀 Producción Final

**Tarea:** Graba tu presentación personal de 30 segundos incluyendo:
- Nombre
- Edad
- De dónde eres
- Dónde vives
- Qué haces (trabajo/estudios)

<Aside type="note">
**Tip:** Practica tu presentación hasta que suene natural, no memorizada. Usa contracciones: "I'm" en lugar de "I am".
</Aside>
