## Módulo 5: Pronombres Personales

### 🎯 Objetivo
Usar los pronombres personales correctamente en contexto real.

### 🧠 Vocabulario: Pronombres

| Pronombre | Significado | Uso |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **I** | Yo | Siempre mayúscula |
| **you** | Tú / Usted / Ustedes | Singular y plural |
| **he** | Él | Hombres |
| **she** | Ella | Mujeres |
| **it** | Eso/Ello | Cosas, animales, clima |
| **we** | Nosotros | Grupo que incluye al hablante |
| **they** | Ellos/Ellas | Grupo de personas o cosas |

### 🧩 Chunks: Pronombres en Contexto

| Pronombre | Frase de ejemplo | 🔊 |
|-----------|------------------|-----|
| I | **I'm hungry** (Tengo hambre) | <AudioButton text="I'm hungry" /> |
| You | **You're right** (Tienes razón) | <AudioButton text="You're right" /> |
| He | **He's my friend** (Es mi amigo) | <AudioButton text="He's my friend" /> |
| She | **She's a doctor** (Ella es doctora) | <AudioButton text="She's a doctor" /> |
| It | **It's cold today** (Hace frío hoy) | <AudioButton text="It's cold today" /> |
| We | **We're ready** (Estamos listos) | <AudioButton text="We're ready" /> |
| They | **They're coming** (Vienen) | <AudioButton text="They're coming" /> |

### 🧩 Chunks: Uso de "IT"

| Situación | Ejemplo | 🔊 |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| Clima | **It's hot** / **It's raining** | <AudioButton text="It's hot. It's raining" /> |

| Distancia | **It's far** / **It's close** | <AudioButton text="It's far. It's close" /> |
| Objetos | **It's on the table** | <AudioButton text="It's on the table" /> |
| Animales | **It's a dog** (mascota sin género) | <AudioButton text="It's a dog" /> |

## Módulo 6: Verbo BE (Uso Práctico)

### 🎯 Objetivo
Usar el verbo BE en afirmaciones, negaciones y preguntas de forma natural.

### 🧠 Vocabulario: Formas del Verbo BE

| Pronombre | Forma completa | Contracción |
|-----------|----------------|-------------|
| I | am | I'm |
| You | are | You're |
| He | is | He's |
| She | is | She's |
| It | is | It's |
| We | are | We're |
| They | are | They're |

### 🧩 Chunks: Afirmaciones con BE

| Categoría | Ejemplos | 🔊 |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| Identidad | I'm Carlos / She's a teacher | <AudioButton text="I'm Carlos. She's a teacher" /> |
| Origen | I'm from Peru / He's from Japan | <AudioButton text="I'm from Peru. He's from Japan" /> |
| Estado | I'm tired / We're hungry | <AudioButton text="I'm tired. We're hungry" /> |
| Ubicación | I'm at home / They're at work | <AudioButton text="I'm at home. They're at work" /> |
| Edad | I'm 25 / She's 30 years old | <AudioButton text="I'm 25. She's 30 years old" /> |
| Descripción | It's big / They're nice | <AudioButton text="It's big. They're nice" /> |

### 🧩 Chunks: Negaciones con BE

| Forma completa | Contracción común |
|----------------|-------------------|
| I am not | I'm not |
| You are not | You're not / You aren't |
| He is not | He's not / He isn't |
| She is not | She's not / She isn't |
| It is not | It's not / It isn't |
| We are not | We're not / We aren't |
| They are not | They're not / They aren't |

**Frases comunes:**
- <AudioButton text="I'm not sure" />
- <AudioButton text="It's not a problem" />
- <AudioButton text="She's not here" />
- <AudioButton text="They aren't ready" />

### 🧩 Chunks: Preguntas con BE

| Estructura | Ejemplo | 🔊 |
|------------|----------|-----|
| Am I...? | Am I late? | <AudioButton text="Am I late?" /> |
| Are you...? | Are you ready? | <AudioButton text="Are you ready?" /> |
| Is he/she/it...? | Is she coming? | <AudioButton text="Is she coming?" /> |
| Are we...? | Are we there yet? | <AudioButton text="Are we there yet?" /> |
| Are they...? | Are they open? | <AudioButton text="Are they open?" /> |

### 🧩 Chunks: Respuestas Cortas

| Pregunta | Respuesta Sí | Respuesta No | 🔊 |
|----------|--------------|--------------|-----|
| Are you ready? | Yes, I am | No, I'm not | <AudioButton text="Are you ready? Yes, I am. No, I'm not" /> |
| Is he your friend? | Yes, he is | No, he isn't | <AudioButton text="Is he your friend? Yes, he is. No, he isn't" /> |
| Are they coming? | Yes, they are | No, they aren't | <AudioButton text="Are they coming? Yes, they are. No, they aren't" /> |
